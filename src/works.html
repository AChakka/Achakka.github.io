Runlotte

Runlotte is an intelligent running route planner that curates optimal paths tailored to your location and preferences. Leveraging real-time data, Runlotte factors in air quality, temperature, weather conditions, elevation, sidewalks, and water fountain locations to ensure a comfortable and safe experience.

Stardaze

StarDaze features an interactive celestial map, immersing users in a 3D view of stars and constellations, positioned as they would appear from their actual location using geocoordinates. This lets users explore their favorite stars and constellations in a realistic simulation of the night sky.
