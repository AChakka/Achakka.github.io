Who am I?

I am a Computer Science student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, minoring in Economics with a focus on Artificial Intelligence. I have a strong foundation in programming languages like Java, Python, and JavaScript, and experience with frameworks and libraries such as React and Node.js. My projects, including Runlotte and StarDaze, showcase my ability to develop innovative solutions using APIs, spatial algorithms, and data visualization. Additionally, I contribute to the Charlotte AI Research Club, working on autonomous racing vehicles with machine learning and computer vision. My passion for learning extends to engaging with real-world challenges, refining my skills in AI, data structures, and software engineering.
